# Automotive-Segment-Case-Study-Real-World-Data-Analysis-
I have to study and analyse how different automotive segments performed from January to June. The study was focused on the customers who visited the showrooms and bought cars. This report also highlights the customers demand and interest and lead conversion pattern.
## Summary
I just have completed a project on Automotive Segment Analysis and it is thrilling to share my work and experience with you. I am happy to work with real world data and make meaningful insight which helps to make organisation for better decisions.
## Objective: -
The main aim of this case study was to:
- Understand visitor behaviour across different automotive segments.
- Identify which segment is growing the fastest.
- Study the relationship between visits and leads.
- Analyse which segment gained more  traction and why this happened.
- Analyse conversion trends and find improvement areas.
- Provide recommendations based on insights.
